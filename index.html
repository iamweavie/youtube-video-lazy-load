Lazy Load YouTube Video & Scripts

The purpose of this pen is to lazy load a video when the user clicks or scrolls the on the video thumbnail. It's like pie, simple, and sweet, but with the secret ingredient: mute=1.

What I have found is in order for the autoplay to work consistently, one needs to add the mute=1 url parameter in addition to autoplay=1. This is the most consistent way to allow a video to autoplay, as there are rules and permissions around autoplay in chrome and other browsers.
